Product Fit and Validation

But most often the demise of startups is the fact they want to pitch the entire product when the customer is looking for one capability. We call that the Swiss Army Knife Effect – I only need a screwdriver – but you want to talk about the knife, the crock screw, and the scissors. We say lets focus on the value of screwdriver – the rest will fall in place.
